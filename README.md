# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analytics | Microsoft Excel Business Intelligence Project

> An interactive Business Intelligence dashboard built in **Microsoft Excel** to analyze coffee shop sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product demand, and store-level performance using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPIs, and Slicers.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end **Sales Analytics Dashboard** developed using Microsoft Excel.

The dashboard transforms raw transactional sales data into meaningful business insights that help management monitor sales performance, customer footfall, product demand, and operational trends across multiple coffee shop locations.

The solution enables stakeholders to make data-driven decisions through dynamic visualizations and interactive filtering.

# 🎯 Business Problem

Coffee shop businesses process thousands of daily transactions, making it difficult to identify sales trends, customer behavior, and operational opportunities without proper analytics.

This dashboard addresses key business challenges by helping stakeholders:

* Monitor overall sales performance
* Analyze customer footfall
* Identify top-selling products
* Compare store performance
* Understand customer purchasing behavior
* Optimize inventory planning
* Improve operational decision-making

---

# 🎯 Project Objective

Develop an interactive Excel dashboard that enables business users to:

* Track overall business performance
* Monitor sales and customer trends
* Analyze product category contribution
* Compare franchise performance

* Support data-driven business decisions

# 🔍 Key Business Insights

## ☕ Coffee

Coffee contributes approximately **39%** of total sales, making it the highest-performing product category.

---

## 🌅 Peak Customer Traffic

Customer orders are concentrated during the morning hours, indicating the importance of efficient staffing during peak periods.

---

## 🏪 Highest Performing Store

Hell's Kitchen is the strongest-performing outlet based on overall revenue generation.

---

## 🥇 Customer Preferences

Espresso-based beverages remain the most popular products among customers.

---

## 📈 Weekly Sales Pattern

Sales remain consistently strong throughout weekdays, with Friday achieving the highest revenue.

---

# 🚀 Business Recommendations

### Improve Peak Hour Operations

* Increase staffing during morning hours.
* Optimize inventory for peak demand.

### Expand High-Performing Products

Focus marketing efforts on:

* Espresso beverages
* Brewed coffee
* Tea products

### Store-Level Optimization

Leverage best practices from Hell's Kitchen to improve performance across other locations.

### Customer Experience

Introduce loyalty programs and promotional campaigns to increase repeat purchases.
